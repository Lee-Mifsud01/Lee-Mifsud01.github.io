RTX 4090

The NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090, unveiled in September 2018, stands as a pinnacle in high-end graphics cards, showcasing the power of the Turing architecture. Boasting 4,352 CUDA cores, 11 GB of GDDR6 memory on a 352-bit bus with a speed of 14 Gbps, and a base clock around 1350 MHz, this GPU delivers exceptional performance for gaming and content creation. Featuring 68 dedicated ray tracing cores and 544 Tensor Cores, the RTX 4090 introduces real-time ray tracing and AI-driven enhancements, including NVIDIA's Deep Learning Super Sampling (DLSS), for unprecedented visual realism and performance. With support for PCIe 3.0, multiple DisplayPort and HDMI outputs, and a power requirement of around 250 watts, the RTX 4090 represents a technological apex in graphics processing, offering an immersive and cutting-edge experience for enthusiasts and professionals alike. Custom models from various manufacturers may further refine the card with unique cooling solutions, overclocking, and additional features.
